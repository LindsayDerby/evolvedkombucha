WHAT IS KOMBUCHA?

Kombucha is an all-natural, lightly effervescent drink made from high-quality botanicals, natural flavors, and loose leaf tea. It is a fermented drink rich in probiotics, antioxidants, and healthy bateria. It has long been renowned for its health benefits and we think it tastes pretty good too.
